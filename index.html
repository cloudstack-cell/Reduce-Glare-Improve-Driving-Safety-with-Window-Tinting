How Window Tinting Reduces Glare

Window tinting minimizes glare by filtering out excessive light while maintaining clear visibility. SolarFX Window Tint is engineered to reduce glare without distorting vision, ensuring a safer driving experience.

SolarFX tints reduce the amount of visible light transmission (VLT), preventing harsh sun rays from blinding drivers.
